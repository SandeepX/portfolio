About Me

I am a passionate Full Stack Developer with over 5 years of professional experience since 2019. My expertise spans a wide range of technologies including PHP, Laravel, CodeIgniter, JavaScript, jQuery, Vue.js, HTML, and CSS. I specialize in API development, third-party integrations, and comprehensive bug fixing solutions.

Throughout my career, I've worked with notable companies in Nepal such as Allpasal, Cyclone Nepal Infotech, Yaztech, and I'm currently employed at Midas Technologies. Beyond coding, I'm an avid sports enthusiast, enjoying games like cricket, table tennis, and chess. I maintain my mental and physical fitness through regular exercise, yoga, gym workouts, hiking, and trekking.<
